My Story

Hope W. Bailey is an up and coming children’s author who has been dedicated towards bettering the lives of children, and this is where her passions have remained.

She has worked as a Social Worker, specializing in Adoption and Fostering for over 30 years and has worked with children from the ages of 3 months to 18 years old. After retraining, she worked as an Educational Trainer, as well as owning and operating a nursery school for children 6 months to 5 years old.

“My work has informed my writing and deepened my understanding of a child’s interior world.”
